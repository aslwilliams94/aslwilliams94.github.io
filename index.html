About

I grew up in Texarkana, Arkansas, and graduated from Yale University in 2016 with a degree in the History of Science--becoming the first person in my family to graduate with a Bachelor's Degree. While at Yale, I received grants and fellowships to study at the University of Siena (Italy) and Oxford University (England), won the Willard “Woody” Brittain, Jr. ’70 Leadership Award, and participated in the arts, multicultural student life, and educational organizations on campus.

Now, I am wrapping up my two-year stint as a Preschool Teaching Fellow at Greenhill School in Addison, Texas. I appreciate my experience giving back through education and now I am excited to embark on my new journey as a web developer.

In my free time I like to work on personal projects, try new restaurants, and explore Dallas with friends and loved ones.
